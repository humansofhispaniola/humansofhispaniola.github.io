ABOUT

Humans of Hispaniola is an organization created to help tell the story of the men, women, and children living in the island of Hispaniola. Haiti and the Dominican Republic share the island just south east of the United States in the Atlantic Ocean. These countries have a long history of clash, and difficulties persist between the people of Haiti and the Dominican Republic. Through an immersion trip, we were able to meet, speak, sing, dance, and accompany so many incredible people. Our mission at Humans of Hispaniola is to make these voices heard.
